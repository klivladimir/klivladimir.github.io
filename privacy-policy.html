Privacy Policy

App: sudo: Sudoku (package com.klivladimir.sudo)
Last updated: 17 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the sudo: Sudoku mobile application (the “App”) handles your information. The App is developed and published by Vladimir Klimovich (the “Developer”).

Summary

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. It works fully offline and contains no accounts, no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal or sensitive user data. We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not request your name, email address, location, contacts, or any device identifiers.

Data Stored on Your Device

To make the game work, the App saves the following locally on your device only:

  • Game progress and current puzzle state
  • App settings and preferences
  • Gameplay statistics and solving-technique progress

This information never leaves your device, is not accessible to the Developer or any third party, and is permanently removed when you uninstall the App or clear its storage.

Network Access

The released App does not request the Internet permission and makes no network connections. No data is sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party advertising, analytics, crash-reporting, or social networking services that would collect your data.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
